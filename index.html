About Me

Hey there, I'm Seth—a true Montana boy through and through. Born and raised in the heart of Big Sky Country, I've always felt most alive exploring the great outdoors, chasing adventure, and spending time with my incredible family (shoutout to my endlessly patient wife, Megan, and our two wild, wonderful kiddos!).

I've spent my life building things, both technical and non-technical, and I absolutely thrive at the intersection of mechanical precision and electrical magic. After more than a decade in aerospace engineering, tinkering with projects that literally touched the sky, I decided to take a leap—trading rocket science for something even riskier: memecoin investing. Yep, you read that right! While some call it madness, I call it strategic chaos, and hey—so far, so good.

When I'm not navigating crypto markets or diving into a DIY project that probably (definitely) got out of hand, you'll find me metal detecting historic sites around Helena, rebuilding trailers, or scheming about my next sustainable cultivation venture (keep an eye out for Roope’s Raw Honey!).

Life's too short not to laugh, explore, and build cool stuff. Welcome to my world—I hope you stick around for the adventure!
